# Famoria Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 24, 2026**
**Effective date: at first launch**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Famoria** (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use the Famoria mobile application (the “App”). This policy applies to all Famoria users, including family members invited to join an existing family group.

**Contact:** laurynsmeester@gmail.com
**Operator:** Famoria — sole proprietorship operated by Lauryn Smeester, United States

---

## 1. The short version

- We collect the data **you** give us (name, email, photos, events, journal entries, etc.) so the app can store and sync it across your family.
- We **do not** sell your data. We **do not** advertise to you. We **do not** profile you for third parties.
- Your data lives in your Firebase project (Google Cloud, US data centers) and is shared only with people **you** invite into your family.
- You can **delete your account and all your personal data** from inside the app at any time: Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account.
- Health data read from Apple Health is shown only on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers.

If anything below conflicts with that summary, the longer text governs.

---

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Information you provide directly

When you create an account or use Famoria, you give us:

- **Account info** — your name, email address, and password (stored hashed by Firebase Authentication).
- **Profile info** — optional profile photo, birth date, and family role (owner, admin, member).
- **Family content** — events you create, photos / videos you upload, posts, journal entries, recipes, documents, wishlist items, medications, health records, and chat messages between family members.
- **Family tree info** — names, relationships, photos, birth dates, and notes for the people you add to your family tree, including relatives who are not Famoria users.
- **Subscription info** — Apple processes the transaction. We receive a receipt confirming the purchase and your subscription tier.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- **Device tokens for push notifications** — when you grant notification permission, Apple Push Notification service issues a token that we store on your user record so the app can send you family pings (event reminders, chat messages, etc.). The token is rotated by iOS and can be cleared by signing out or deleting the app.
- **Approximate language and time-zone** — read from your device to localize dates and reminders.
- **Basic crash and performance signals** — if/when Crashlytics is enabled, we collect anonymized crash stacks, model, OS version, and language. No personal identifiers.

### 2.3 Information we do NOT collect

- **Apple Health data** — Famoria reads steps, sleep, and resting heart rate from HealthKit purely on-device to display in the Family Health Center. **No HealthKit data ever leaves your device.** We do not upload it, log it, or share it.
- **Contacts** — if you grant contacts access, we use it only to display your contacts in the invite picker. We do not upload, copy, or store your address book.
- **Location** — we use Apple’s MapKit to render a preview of an event location you type. We do not request continuous location and we do not store your location.
- **Advertising identifiers** — we do not collect or use IDFA.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

- Create and maintain your Famoria account and family.
- Sync your family’s shared content (events, photos, chats, etc.) across the devices of people you have invited into your family.
- Deliver push notifications you have opted into (event reminders, chat messages, RSVP updates).
- Process subscription transactions through Apple and unlock paid features for your family.
- Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and security issues.
- Comply with our legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

We do **not** use your data to train AI models, sell advertising, profile you across services, or sell to data brokers.

---

## 4. How we share your information

We share your information only as follows:

- **Within your family** — content you create in a family (events, posts, photos, chats, etc.) is visible to the other members of that family, scoped by the privacy controls you choose per item (private / family / specific members).
- **Service providers** — we rely on Google Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging) for storage and infrastructure, and Apple for payment processing and push delivery. These providers process the data on our behalf and are contractually bound to handle it only for our service.
- **Legal compliance** — we may disclose information when required by law, subpoena, or to protect Famoria’s rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
- **Business transfer** — if Famoria is acquired or merged, your information may transfer to the new owner, subject to this policy.

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

---

## 5. Where your data is stored

Famoria stores your data in **Google Cloud (Firebase)** US-Central data centers. By using Famoria you consent to your data being processed in the United States.

---

## 6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account):

- Your Firebase Authentication account is deleted.
- Your user document, personal tasks, notifications addressed to you, and avatar are deleted.
- Your name and contributions to **shared family content** (posts you made, events you created, photos you uploaded) remain available to the family members you shared them with, attributed to your free-text name, so the rest of your family does not lose collaborative work.

If you want shared content removed too, please contact privacy@famoria.app from your account email and we will assist within 30 days.

---

## 7. Your choices

- **Account deletion**: Profile → Privacy & Security → Delete Account.
- **Notification controls**: iOS Settings → Famoria → Notifications, or in-app Profile → Notifications.
- **Health, Contacts, Photos**: iOS Settings → Famoria → toggle each permission.
- **Subscriptions**: managed in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions.
- **Data export**: contact privacy@famoria.app for a JSON export of your data.

---

## 8. Children

Famoria is intended for adults setting up family groups, not for children to register independently. If you are a parent adding a child to your family tree, you are responsible for the child’s information. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, contact privacy@famoria.app and we will delete the account.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (Google Cloud-managed encryption). Firebase Authentication hashes passwords. Firestore security rules restrict reads and writes to authenticated family members. We do not store payment card data — Apple handles all subscription billing.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact privacy@famoria.app immediately.

If you are in California, the EEA, UK, or another jurisdiction with privacy rights, you may have additional rights including the right to access, correct, delete, or port your information, and the right to opt out of certain processing. Contact privacy@famoria.app and we will respond within the time required by applicable law.

We have not enabled the Global Privacy Control because we do not sell personal information.
